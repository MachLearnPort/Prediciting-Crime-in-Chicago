Chapter 1. Magic Squares Explained

The German artist Albrecht Durer (1471-1528) created many woodcuts and prints with religious and scientific symbolism. One of his most famous works, Melancholia I, explores the depressed state of mind that opposes inspiration and expression. Renaissance astrologers believed that the Jupiter magic square (shown in the upper right portion of the image) could aid in the cure of melancholy.
